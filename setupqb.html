Set up a question bank

How to set up a question bank for your medical school.

KCL Student Question Bank has been successful because it is tailored specifically for the King's College London course. We want students at other medical schools to benefit from this model, but we need your help to make the content relevant to your course.

We are looking for people to become site leaders for other medical schools. We will provide a question bank website with full access to our database of questions from King's. As a site leader, you will build a question bank for your course, using a combination of our questions and those you write yourself. You don't need any programming skills at all. This is a great way to revise for your exams, we will give you a certificate for your CV, and of course we will pay you - you will receive 50% of all profits received from people signing up to your question bank. Marketing the site to your fellow students is also up to you - the more people you can get to sign up, the more money you will make.

If you like the idea of earning money whilst revising, drop us a line at mail@studentquestionbank.com. We look forward to working with you.
