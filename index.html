Student Question Banks

Online questions for YOUR medical school

Studentquestionbank.com offers databases of revision questions written specifically for your course, by students who have done your course.

Practicing questions is widely accepted as the best way to prepare for the multiple choice exams used by most medical schools. Large companies such as Pastest and OnExamination offer excellent online databases for medical finals and beyond. But they aren't perfect - preclinical topics aren't well covered and they don't know how your course is organised, and what your lecturers are likely to ask about.

In 2010 we built KCL Student Question Bank to address these problems. Questions were written by KCL students and linked to specific lectures or topics. After four years we have over 5000 questions covering all aspects of the course (clinical and preclinical), and over a thousand students have used the site.

We want to expand this very successful model to other medical schools. We need enthusiastic medical students to help with this, who will share any profits made. If you are interested in setting up a student question bank please click below to learn more.
